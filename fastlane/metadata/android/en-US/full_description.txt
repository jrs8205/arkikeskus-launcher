Arkikeskus Launcher is a modern, lightweight Android home-screen launcher built entirely with Jetpack Compose and Material 3. It is bilingual (Finnish and English) and works as a standalone launcher for anyone.

Features:

* Paged home screen with free icon placement and smooth drag-and-drop — move icons within a page, across pages, and between the home grid and the dock.
* Dock of favourite apps.
* App drawer with universal search: apps, system settings pages, contacts (opt-in), and a calculator / unit converter, plus an optional most-used apps row.
* Home-screen widgets — add, resize, move, reconfigure, and stretch a widget to full width. Collection widgets (chat lists, calendars) scroll in place.
* Folders on the home screen and inside the app drawer.
* Third-party icon packs (ADW/Nova appfilter format) and Material You themed icons.
* Configurable gestures: swipe up for the app drawer, swipe down for notifications, and a configurable left-edge swipe.
* Notification dots / badges, and an optional themed status bar.
* Backup and restore your layout to a file, plus optional Google Drive auto-backup. Widgets are included in backups.
* Lock desktop, hide apps, rename apps with custom labels, and a self-contained settings screen.

Free and open-source under the Apache-2.0 license.
